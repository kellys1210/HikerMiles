Frequent-Hiker-Miles Database Tables

  • Brain_Implants:
    Tracks details about each hiker's brain implant, including implant ID, installation date, and status.

  • Parks:
    Contains records of all participating public lands, including names, locations, and ranger station status.

  • Patron_Parks:
    Links hikers to the parks they frequent, tracking their visits.

  • Patron_Trails:
    Records the trails hiked by each participant, including distance.

  • Patrons:
    Stores personal information of hikers, including name, address, and brain implant identification.

  • Rewards_Points:
    Tracks the rewards points earned by each hiker based on their miles and rewards redeemed.

  • Trails:
    Maintains data on all trails in the system including distance, trail name, coordinates, and park association.
